# Masterworks Academy

Research and thought leadership platform designed to establish Masterworks as the definitive authority on art market performance, returns, and investing.

## Overview

Art as an asset class suffers from an information asymmetry problem — most investors don't understand how art markets work, how returns are generated, or how to evaluate performance. Masterworks Academy fills that gap while building brand credibility and driving investor acquisition.

**Positioning:** The only research platform purpose-built to help investors understand art market performance and returns — powered by Masterworks' proprietary database of 50M+ auction records and 15,000+ artists tracked.

Modeled after [Apollo Academy](https://www.apollo.com/apollo-academy) (Apollo Global's education hub for financial advisors), it combines rigorous research with accessible education.

## Target Audiences

| Audience | Goal |
|---|---|
| **Existing Investors** | Deepen engagement, reduce churn, increase secondary market activity |
| **Prospective Investors** | Build credibility, educate on art as asset class, drive sign-ups |
| **Financial Advisors & RIAs** | Position Masterworks as a legitimate alternative allocation |
| **Media & Influencers** | Earn coverage, establish Masterworks voices as go-to experts |

## Content Pillars

1. **Research Reports** — Art Market Index (quarterly), market updates (monthly), portfolio updates, artist dossiers
2. **Data & Indices** — Publicly accessible indices (contemporary, post-war, ultra-contemporary), downloadable datasets, transparent methodology
3. **Opinions & Explainers** — Art market mechanics, Masterworks explainers, timely market commentary, hot takes on artist markets
4. **News Aggregation** — Daily digest covering art market and financial news with editorial commentary connecting them ("The Daily Brushstroke")
5. **Cultural Updates** — Art world news commentary, company milestones, artist spotlights

## Distribution

- **Academy Hub** — Standalone section on masterworks.com (or academy.masterworks.com)
- **Email** — Daily weekday digest + Sunday weekly recap
- **LinkedIn** — Research highlights, data charts, expert voice posts (daily)
- **X/Twitter** — Real-time auction reactions, data visualizations, hot takes (multiple daily)
- **Instagram** — Visual-first content, artist spotlight reels, data infographics (3-5x/week)
- **Earned Media** — Syndication partnerships (Bloomberg, CNBC, Barron's), press-ready research, rapid-response commentary

## AI-Powered Content Workflow

Content generation is AI-assisted using internal Masterworks data:

- **Data access** — Internal Redshift tables and a Masterworks MCP server provide Claude/AI tools access to proprietary research data
- **Content generation** — Anyone can contribute ideas; AI generates charts, drafts, and even motion graphics/videos from the data
- **Review pipeline** — Research team approves data accuracy, content team approves presentation
- **Video** — AI-generated motion graphics for social media (artist market visualizations, data stories)

## Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1–3)
- Employees posting to LinkedIn and Masterworks social accounts
- AI-generated videos and materials for social distribution
- Build Academy hub on masterworks.com
- Launch Art Market Index v1
- Publish first 5–10 evergreen explainers
- Establish email newsletter (weekly, building to daily)

### Phase 2: Expansion (Months 4–6)
- Artist dossier series (2/month)
- Monthly market updates tied to auction calendar
- Podcast or video series with expert voices
- First quarterly white paper for advisors
- Media outreach campaign around Index
- Explore CE credit accreditation for advisor content

### Phase 3: Authority (Months 7–12)
- Annual "State of the Art Market" report
- Open data access / API for journalists and academics
- Syndication deals with financial media
- Advisor-specific course (Apollo Academy model)
- First Masterworks Academy event
- Measure and optimize: citations, media mentions, lead attribution
